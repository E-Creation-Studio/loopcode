About Us

LoopCode Traxx - short story

LoopCode Traxx is one of the first Polish virtual/digital labels. Founded in 2000 in Warka (Poland) by DJ, producer, music lover - Phonk.
Label presents mainly acid techno, tech house and tribal house.
The idea to create a virtual label was born at a time when young Polish producers found it difficult to break through with their music among DJs, who still had big objections to playing from a medium other than vinyl.
LoopCode Traxx returns from a deep sleep, releasing recordings from which its whole story began.
